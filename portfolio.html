Studio Chintala Office Bengaluru

Studio Chintala is a multi-disciplinary design studio based in San Fransico and Bangalore. The studio is focused on designing simple and honest spaces and objects, driven purely by functional requirements. The design process involves challenging preconceived notions about buildings and typologies and starting with a “clean-state” and developing concepts based on logic, context and the aspirations of the users. “Styles and Aesthetics” are purely a consequence of the creative process.
